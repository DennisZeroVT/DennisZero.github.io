My First Song: Snowfall

In 2025, I released my first song on music platforms like Spotify. The song was originally made for a friend named eri_k416, also known as Erik. The track was called Snowfall, and it was written about his character named Viona. It was also the first time I ever wrote lyrics for a song, which made it a very special project for me.

Behind the Song

Snowfall was inspired by Viona, a character belonging to my friend Erik. The song represents calmness, winter emotions, and the feeling of purpose.
